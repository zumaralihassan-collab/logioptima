Strategic insights

Mode choice is value-vs-weight
Trucks dominate by value and weight (~73% / ~65%). Pipeline & rail over-index on weight (cheap dense bulk). Air is the inverse — <1% of weight, big share of value.
Crude = pipeline then tanker
Pipeline ~$5/bbl vs rail $10–15 and truck $20. Internationally it shifts to marine tankers.
Expedite trades money for time
Air ~10x ocean per kg but ~10x faster. Express adds ~1.5–2.5x. Ship only high-value/urgent by air.
